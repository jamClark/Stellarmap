Domain Map Viewer

This is the heart and soul of domain layouts. Each room in your file can be placed here to visually indicate where it lies in logical correlation to your domain. Rooms can be created, removed, connected to each other, and moved around using the mouse and keyboard.

The Compass


A yellow compass indicates the origin point of the domain space and should be used to orient your rooms. The View->MoveToOrigin menu option can be used to return to this point should you ever get lost.

Controls

  • Create new room - right Click an empty area and select 'Add Room'
  • Delete a room - right click on a room icon and select 'Delete Room'
  • Move a room - Hold left mouse button while the cursor is over a room to drag it
  • Move a group of rooms - hold left button in an empty space to drag a selection rectangle over multiple rooms. Then drag any selected room as normal and the whole group will follow
  • Connect one room to another - hold left over the small tab that corresponds to an exit. Drag the line out to another room and release. The tab should become hilighted when successful.
  • Move the view - Use the mouse wheel to move vertically and hold shift to move horizontally. Holding the mouse wheel button or Ctrl down while moving the mouse will also work.
